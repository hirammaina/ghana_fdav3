New Directive Regarding Payment Remittance

Starting from tomorrow, July 10th, 2023, all payments for Regulatory Services to Rwanda FDA must be processed using the iREMBO Payment Gateway. As a result, we kindly ask you to create the Request for Payment (Invoice) through the Self-service Portal. This notification serves as a reminder of the new payment procedure.
Note: Other form of payments might not be accepted.
Thank you for your attention to this matter.
